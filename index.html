ROOMS
OUT OF ROOMS

What Rooms should have been.

ROOMS: OUT OF ROOMS is a Roblox game that builds upon the legacy of the iconic Rooms by Nicorocks5555, offering a fresh evolution of the original concept.

Game Features

  • A thousand procedually generated rooms.
  • Each run will be different.
  • Multiple entities to stop you from your adventure.

ROOMS: OUT OF ROOMS challenges you to explore, survive, and uncover the mysteries hidden within the office. You were once a worker but took a break, not knowing the events that happend in the office. Lore can be found in-game.

This game is not related in any way to the official Rooms Universe. This game was made with love and passion, we hope you have a fun time playing!
